LaunchBoard policies

Community Guidelines

Be truthful, respectful, and useful. Do not post spam, scams, impersonation, harassment, stolen material, dangerous content, or intentionally misleading links.

Reviews and messages

Reviews should describe genuine experiences. Messages must not contain threats, unwanted promotions, or abusive content.

Enforcement

Content may be removed and accounts may be limited or banned when these guidelines are violated.
