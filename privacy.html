Privacy Policy

LaunchBoard stores account information, profiles, product listings, messages, reviews, reports, and usage data needed to operate the service.

Service providers

Authentication, database, and file storage are provided through Supabase. Website hosting is provided through GitHub Pages.

Your choices

Users may update profile information and request support regarding account or content removal.

Security

No online service can guarantee absolute security. Users should choose strong, unique passwords and protect access to their email accounts.
